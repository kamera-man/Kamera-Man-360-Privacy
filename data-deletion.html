Data Deletion Request

Studio 360° by Kamera-Man

Studio 360° does not collect or store any user data on external servers.

All data (photos, conversions, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

How to Delete Your Data

To Delete All App Data:

  1. Delete the app from your device
  2. This automatically removes all locally stored data including:
    • Converted 360° photos
    • App preferences
    • Cached thumbnails

Why No Server Deletion is Needed

  • We do not operate servers that store your data
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage
  • All processing happens entirely on your device

Facebook Login Data

If you used Facebook login through our app:

  • We only receive a temporary access token to post on your behalf
  • We do not store your Facebook credentials
  • To revoke access: Go to Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove "Studio 360"
